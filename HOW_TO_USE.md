# How to Run JobHunter

## Step 1 — Download the file
Click on `JobHunter.html` in this repo → click the download button (⬇️)

## Step 2 — Open in browser
Double-click the downloaded `JobHunter.html` file.
It will open in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox — no internet needed.

## Step 3 — Start tracking
- Click **"Log application"** on the dashboard to add your first job
- Use **Job Search** tab to browse matched jobs and apply
- Use **HR Emails** tab to generate personalised outreach emails
- Check **Analytics** to see your progress

## Tips
- Set your **daily goal** in Settings (default: 10 applications/day)
- Use **"Apply + Track"** on any job card — it opens the company site AND logs it
- Export your data regularly: Settings → Export JSON

## Data backup
Your data is stored in your browser. To back it up:
`Settings → Export JSON → Save the file`
